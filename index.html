About Us

The Quest Scholar Network here at Columbia strives to create a space and support system for any and all low-income students attending Columbia. Through different events, initiatives, and other forms of programming, we hope to create a dialogue for low-income students that'll enable us to have a better experience here at Columbia. Our chapter is open to all students of Columbia University, so long as they support our mission of creating a space and support system for low-income students on campus.
